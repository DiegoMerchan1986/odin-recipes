Hot dog

Variation on a hot dog. Kid approved. Experiment and use whatever combo of ingredients you wish.

Prep:10 mins / Cook:20 mins / Total:30 mins / Servings:8 / Yield:8 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs, chopped
  • 2/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons pickle relish
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
  • 3 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 8 hot dog buns

Directions

  1. Preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Stir the hot dogs, Cheddar cheese, relish, ketchup, mustard, and onion together in a bowl; spoon into the hot dog buns. Wrap each sandwich in aluminum foil.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef. Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper. In a separate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until hot, about 20 minutes. Serve immediately.
